Who We Are

Powering Precision in Pump Technology

Mariushi Pumps is a dynamic UAE-based company leading the way in Pump Systems & Solutions. We specialize in the design, supply, installation, servicing, and repair of all types of pumping equipment across the UAE and GCC.

We are brand-agnostic and support all major global pump manufacturers. Our expertise spans pump repairs, overhauling, commissioning, system optimization, consultancy, AMCs, site surveys, vibration analysis, and laser alignment.We offer a comprehensive and specialized service in Supply, as well as the delivery of water, wastewater & other fluids through our expert installation and project management capabilities.

With operations across UAE, Oman, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Bahrain, and Kuwait, we are trusted long-term partners for government, utility, industrial, and private sector clients.

Mariushi Pumps takes pride in offering a responsive and agile service, while delivering consistently high-quality work supported by well-established systems. Simplify your supply chain by sourcing your fluid transfer equipment products and services from one company - Mariushi Pumps.

CORE COMPETENCIES

With a large customer base in utilities, general industry, construction and manufacturing, we provide specialist pump knowledge, agile and responsive service that is brand agnostic.

PROJECT MANAGEMENT

Our project teams specialize in the design, construction and commissioning of water and wastewater infrastructure. Working closely with stakeholders, we deliver quality turnkey solutions and ensure our projects are completed efficiently, safely and within budget.

SERVICE

Mariushi Pump service team specializes in the repair and maintenance of pumping and related equipment like pipe lines, panels and VFDs. Our broad range of services include preventative and breakdown maintenance backed by our fully equipped service team and workshop with the latest technology to ensure our clients satisfaction.
